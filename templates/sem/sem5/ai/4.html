Shuffle Deck of Cards
In the program, we used the product() function in itertools module to create a deck of cards. This function performs the Cartesian product of the two sequences.
Code: 
            
                #import
                import itertools,random

                # initaliazing list of all the cards from 1 to 14 for all the four categories
                deck=list(itertools.product((range(1,14)),['Space','Heart','Diamond','Club']))

                # shuffling the deck list
                random.shuffle(deck)

                #printing the first five cards of the deck
                print("You got:")
                for i in range(5):
                    print(deck[i][0],"of",deck[i][1])
Output: 
            
                You got:
                6 of Heart
                12 of Club
                6 of Diamond
                7 of Diamond
                4 of Heart
