Celsius to Faranite
Write a script that asks users for the temperature in F and prints the temperature in C. (Conversion: Celsius = (F - 32) * 5/9).
Code: 
            
                F = float(input("Enter temperature in in faranite: "))
                Celsius = (F - 32) * 5/9
                print("Temperature in Celsius = ", Celsius)
