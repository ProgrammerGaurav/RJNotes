Area of Triangle
Here s is the semi-perimeter and a, b and c are three sides of the triangle. Area of a triangle = (s*(s-a)*(s-b)*(s-c))-1/2
Code: 
            
                a = int(input("Enter side A : "))
                b = int(input("Enter side B : "))
                c = int(input("Enter side C : "))
                s = (a+b+c)/2
                area = (s*(s-a)*(s-b)*(s-c)) ** 0.5
                print("Area of triangle = ", area)
Output: 
            
                Enter side A :  4
                Enter side B :  3
                Enter side C :  5
                Area of triangle =  6.0
